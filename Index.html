History

Galgudd's history can be organized into three time periods: the King's Origin, the Great War, and the Era of Benevolence. Learn about the history of Galgudd!

The King's Origin

When our good King Adelaide was a child, the great kingdom of Glagudd was in ruins. Barbarians from the North and pirates from the South had ravaged our borders, leaving us at the mercy of scoundrels. However, Adelaide knew that they could restore the period to greatness with their magical talent and wits.

The Great War

King Adelaide, still a child with nothing to their name, organized an uprising. With their boundless charm and infectous spirit, they brought millions to their side and the war began.

The Era of Benevolence

After three bloody years of fighting, King Adelaide made peace with the invaders. All sides settled down in harmony with newly forged borders and a healthy trading relationship. This is the golden age of Galgudd, known as The Era of Benevolence, as the people crowned Adelaide king.

LEGENDS

Now that you know the history, its time to talk about Galgudd's prevalent legends! Bardic tradition is the backbone of Galgudd, as it is customary for families to pass stories through the generations through performances and readings.

The Forest Lantern

The Forest Lantern appears when you are lost and keeps you safe, leading you through the dark into a new light.

The Dice of Fate

The Dice of Fate are colorful dice that are rolled at the moment of your death to see what afterlife you go to (if you go to one at all).

The Infinite Cosmos

The Infinite Cosmos is our oldest legend. It's said that early travelers jumped across the stars to forge their path.
